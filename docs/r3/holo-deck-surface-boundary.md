# Holo-Deck Surface Boundary

## Purpose

The Holo-Deck page is a shielded constraint-test surface for Wave-II Alpha. It makes the Holo-Deck doctrine visible in the cockpit shell without adding execution capability or raw vault access.

## Page role

Holo-Deck tests judgment under constraints.

It surfaces:

- Scenario Packet
- Rulebook-as-Physics
- Blind Answer Seal
- Run State
- Death-Zone Monitor
- Failure Projection
- Promotion Tracker
- Ipcha Mistabra Trial

## Boundary

The page is UI/routing only.

It must not:

- execute trades
- route orders
- connect broker transport
- store credentials
- browse raw Sarlaac or MSP memory
- certify Algorilla readiness
- promote contaminated runs
- display fake pass probability
- imply that stale or degraded truth is actionable

## Doctrine

Holo-Deck may nominate. MDK certifies.

W³ may judge. MSP stores. Scythe harvests. Algorilla hardens.

If the surviving decision is WAIT, REJECT, or DO NOT TRADE, that is a correct output.

## Ipcha Mistabra insertion

Every Holo-Deck candidate must face two trials:

- Trial of Light: what supports the claim?
- Trial of Darkness: what kills, contradicts, or inverts the claim?

A claim that survives only supporting evidence is not truth. A claim must withstand the darkness and the light before it may move toward MDK verification.
